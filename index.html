What does the handmade web look like?

List of thinks that, in my opinion, evoke the handmade web:

  • Blue hyperlinks
  • Text-heavy webpages
  • Gifs
  • Emoji and ascii art
  • 'Naive', 'Amateur', 'Unpolished' design sensibility.
  • FUN
  • Some lack of focus in content
  • Lack of attempts to monentise content
  • Inconsistencies between pages
  • Errors, broken links, images that load slowly or not at all
  • Ephemerality
